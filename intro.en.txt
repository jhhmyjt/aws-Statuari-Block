[b]A simple mod that add a new beautiful practical stone block called statuari(rgb 255,254,249) in
    game, you can use
    any chunk to make it. Besides, You can use statuari block to make drug lab, tailor
    bench, hi-tech research bench etc in vanilla.[/b]

[b]Works with existing saves.[/b]
Cannot be removed from saves easily.

[b]Compatibility[/b]
Royalty - Fine tile
ideology - Styling station\Skullspike\Gibbet cage
[url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2464923082]Refined floors (discontinued)[/url] - stone floors
[url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1817862464][WD] Expanded Floors[/url] - stone floors
